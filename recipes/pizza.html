Pizza

Pizza is a popular Italian dish consisting of a round, flat base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomatoes, cheese, and often various other ingredients (such as anchovies, olives, vegetables, meat, etc.), which is then baked at a high temperature, traditionally in a wood-fired oven.

Description:

This pizza recipe features a crispy crust topped with a rich tomato sauce, melted mozzarella cheese, and fresh basil. It's a simple yet delicious meal that can be customized with your favorite toppings.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 packet (2 1/4 teaspoons) active dry yeast
  • 3/4 cup warm water (110째F or 45째C)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Toppings of your choice (pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, etc.)
  • Olive oil for drizzling

Instructions

  1. In a small bowl, dissolve the sugar in warm water and sprinkle the yeast on top. Let it sit for about 5 minutes until frothy.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour and salt. Make a well in the center and add the yeast mixture and olive oil.
  3. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5-7 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  4. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover with a damp cloth, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour or until doubled in size.
  5. Preheat your oven to 475째F (245째C).
  6. Punch down the risen dough and roll it out on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  7. Transfer the rolled dough to a pizza stone or baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  8. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  9. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce, then add your desired toppings.
  10. Drizzle with olive oil and bake in the preheated oven for about 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden brown and cheese is bubbly.
  11. Remove from the oven, garnish with fresh basil leaves, slice, and serve hot.

Enjoy your homemade pizza!
