Hamburger

A hamburger is a classic American dish consisting of a ground beef patty served in a sliced bun, often accompanied by various toppings such as lettuce, tomato, cheese, pickles, onions, and condiments like ketchup and mustard.

Description:

This hamburger recipe features a juicy beef patty seasoned to perfection, topped with fresh lettuce, ripe tomato, crispy pickles, and melted cheese, all nestled in a soft sesame seed bun. It's a simple yet satisfying meal that can be customized with your favorite toppings.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef (80% lean)
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 4 hamburger buns
  • 4 slices of cheese (optional)
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Tomato slices
  • Pickles
  • Ketchup and mustard (to taste)
  • Onion slices (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your grill or stovetop skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. In a bowl, gently mix the ground beef with salt and pepper. Avoid overmixing to keep the patties tender.
  3. Divide the mixture into 4 equal portions and shape them into patties, making a slight indentation in the center of each patty to prevent puffing during cooking.
  4. Place the patties on the grill or skillet and cook for about 4-5 minutes on one side.
  5. Flip the patties and cook for an additional 3-4 minutes, adding cheese slices on top if desired, until the cheese is melted and the patties reach your preferred level of doneness.
  6. Toast the hamburger buns on the grill or in a toaster until lightly golden.
  7. Assemble the burgers by placing a patty on each bun, followed by lettuce, tomato, pickles, onions, and your choice of condiments.
  8. Serve immediately and enjoy your homemade hamburgers!

Enjoy your delicious homemade hamburger!
