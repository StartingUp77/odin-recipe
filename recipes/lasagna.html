Lasagna

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat, cheese, and sauce. It's a hearty meal that is perfect for family gatherings or special occasions.

Description:

This lasagna recipe features a rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta cheese, and gooey mozzarella, all layered between sheets of pasta. It's baked to perfection and garnished with fresh basil for an extra touch of flavor.

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a skillet, brown the ground beef over medium heat. Drain excess fat.
  4. Add marinara sauce to the skillet with the beef and simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, oregano, salt, and pepper until well combined.
  6. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a baking dish.
  7. Layer 3 lasagna noodles over the sauce, then spread half of the ricotta mixture over the noodles.
  8. Add a layer of meat sauce, followed by a layer of mozzarella cheese.
  9. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of meat sauce and mozzarella cheese on top.
  10. Cover with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes until cheese is bubbly and golden.
  11. Let it cool for a few minutes before serving. Garnish with fresh basil if desired.

Enjoy your homemade lasagna!
